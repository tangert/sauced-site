About

We are a few Emory students who are passionate about good food. Today, we introduce Sauced, a pseudo supper club for people who want to eat well and escape the daily routine of fast food and fast conversations.

Sauced is a community where friends can get together, cook together, hang out, and most importantly, feel at home.
